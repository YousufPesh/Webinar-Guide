The Scenario

The IT help desk receives a request to create a distribution list for a special assignment in PSYCH 3012.

Previously, instructors submitted requests via email, manually shared group details, and waited for IT staff to log into the email system and configure the list. This process was slow and required unnecessary back-and-forth.

How ClassBuddy Responds

Step 1 – Intake the Request

An instructor submits the distribution list request through ClassBuddy:

"I need a distribution list for a special assignment in PSYCH 3012."

ClassBuddy collects all required metadata and creates a ServiceNow ticket automatically:

  • Purpose: Survey distribution for PSYCH 3012
  • Request Type: Distribution list
  • Department: Psychology
  • Urgency and due date

The IT team sees a fully detailed request without needing to follow up for missing information.

Step 2 – Define the Distribution Group

ClassBuddy prompts:

"To create a DL, I'll need the following info:
DL Name (required)
Public or Private? (required)
DL email alias (must be unique) (required)
Description (optional — you can leave it blank)"

The instructor replies:

"distribution group: psych_study_202506_mable
visibility: public
email alias: ai_use_survey
description: ai distribution group to capture survey results"

ClassBuddy records the group name and membership rules, then allows the IT team to review and create the group directly within ClassBuddy or approve automated provisioning.

Step 3 – Retrieve Survey Data

Once the list is active and survey responses are collected, the instructor follows up:

"Show me the current survey results."

ClassBuddy connects to the integrated survey platform and returns:

  • A direct link to detailed results

IT no longer needs to manually generate exports or reports.
