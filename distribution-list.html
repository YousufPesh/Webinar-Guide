The Scenario

An instructor is preparing a special assignment for the Psychology course PSYCH 3012 and needs a dedicated distribution list to manage student responses for a case study.

Previously, the instructor had to submit a manual IT request, provide group details over email, and wait for IT staff to log into the email system to configure the list.

How ClassBuddy Responds

Step 1 – Initiate the Request

The instructor tells ClassBuddy:

"I need a distribution list for a special assignment in PSYCH 3012."

ClassBuddy asks a few clarifying questions and creates a ServiceNow ticket with the appropriate metadata:

  • Purpose: Survey distribution for PSYCH 3012
  • Request Type: Distribution list
  • Department: Psychology
  • Urgency and due date

Step 2 – Define the Distribution Group

ClassBuddy prompts:

"What would you like to name the distribution group?"

The instructor replies:

"PSYCH3012_survey_test"

ClassBuddy records the group name and any member criteria, then passes this to the automated provisioning workflow.

Step 3 – Retrieve Survey Data

Once the distribution list is created and used for outreach, the instructor can return and ask:

"Show me the current survey results."

ClassBuddy connects to the linked survey platform and returns a visual summary or a direct link to results.
